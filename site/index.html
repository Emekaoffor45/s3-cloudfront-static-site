About

A bit about me.

I studied Computer Science at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka. A lot of my coursework and research was in computer networks, network security, and distributed cloud systems, so infrastructure has always been the part I found most interesting.

My first real job was at Tizeti, configuring routers, switches, VPNs and VLANs, and helping deploy a solar-powered base station that brought Wi-Fi to a rural community. After that I built full-stack applications at OurPass Digital Bank, and right now I handle IT support, QA, and project delivery at Ideation Platform.

Cloud and DevOps felt like the obvious next step for me. It's the same thing I've always cared about, keeping systems running and shipping reliably, just done properly: automate the manual parts, make deployments repeatable, and keep the infrastructure in version control. I'm learning it by building things and putting them online, which you can see below.
